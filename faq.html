Is this investment advice?

No. AlphaStocks.ai is a product demo and ritual companion. It helps you organize research and accountability loops, but it does not provide brokerage, execution, or personalized advice.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Install the Progressive Web App and the service worker will cache the workspace shell, recent notes, and ledger highlights so you can review them when traveling.

Which devices are supported?

Any modern Chromium, Safari, or Firefox browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Safe-area padding keeps things usable on devices with display cutouts.

Can I invite a team?

The public demo ships with a single-seat flow. Reach out if you'd like to experiment with shared rituals, Supabase sync, or custom data feeds.

How do updates ship?

The service worker performs background refreshes. When new assets are available they're fetched silently and applied the next time you open the app.

Where can I send feedback?

Contact hello@alphastocks.ai with bugs, feature ideas, or integration requests.

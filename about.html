Designed for real-life trading sessions

  • Offline-first. Launch the PWA on your phone, hop on a flight, and keep reviewing ideas without connectivity.
  • Fast context switching. The dashboard, alpha tracker, ledger, and daily check-ins live in one tap-friendly shell.
  • Safe-area aware. iOS and Android status bars are handled automatically so nothing feels cramped.

What's inside

Morning dashboard

Pre-market checklists, market internals, and timelines to keep you honest about the day's focus.

Alpha notebook

Capture hypotheses, catalysts, and conviction levels, then promote them into your punchcard stack.

Portfolio ledger

Ledger highlights and results tiles stay cached so you can reference them even when you're offline.

Availability

AlphaStocks.ai runs anywhere a modern browser is available. Install it as a Progressive Web App on mobile, tablet, or desktop to keep session state, local preferences, and cached data at your fingertips.
